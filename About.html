About Us

Company Background

Nomo Trading and Projects is a 100% black-owned, private registered company established in 2011. The company proudly boasts 50% woman shareholding, reflecting its commitment to inclusivity and empowerment.

Nomo Trading and Projects is located in Malelane, Mpumalanga Province, operating under the Nkomazi Local Municipality. The company specializes in Construction and Cleaning Services, delivering dependable and professional solutions to both public and private sector clients.

Our Vision

To be one of the major contributors in the construction and oil industries, recognized for quality, reliability, and sustainable development.

Our Mission

To provide excellent, high-quality, and cost-effective services while strictly adhering to environmental and safety regulations, and ensuring our employees are well cared for at all times.

Our Values

  • Health, Safety and Environment
  • Reliability
  • Customer Centric Approach
  • Quality Service Delivery
  • Integrity
  • Employee Wellbeing
  • Community Development
